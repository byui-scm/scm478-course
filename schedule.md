## Unit Overview

- **Unit 1 (Weeks 1-4): Peak Fuel Dashboard.** Data visibility, multi-source lookup, shortage warning, SQLite. Practice company: Peak Fuel Foods. Transfer Exam: Kunzler's Supply Chain Dashboard (different company).
- **Unit 2 (Weeks 5-7): Forecasting Module.** Forecasting methods, accuracy metrics, archiving. Transfer Exam: Bakery scenario.
- **Unit 3 (Weeks 8-10): Constraint-Based Planning + Inventory.** Identify the constraint, product-mix priorities, BOM explosion, inventory buffers. Transfer Exam: Furniture shop.
- **Unit 4 (Weeks 11-12): Flow Control and Purchasing.** Drum-Buffer-Rope scheduling, replenishment tied to the constraint. Transfer Exam: Broken flow system.
- **Unit 5 (Week 13): Integration + Capstone.** Mega Exam (Kunzler's cafe, different company), Oral Demonstration, Architecture Document.
