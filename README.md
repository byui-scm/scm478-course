## About This Course

You will build a supply chain management system for **Peak Fuel Foods**, a small company that makes protein burritos, bowls, wraps, bars, and smoothie packs. The founder, Maria Solano, manages her supply chain with Google Sheets and her memory. Your job is to build something better.

Over 13 weeks, you will construct an application that helps Maria:

- **See her data** -- products, ingredients, suppliers, sales, inventory
- **Spot problems** -- shortage warnings, late suppliers, demand shifts  
- **Make decisions** -- what to produce, what to order, from whom, and when

You don't need to know how to code. **AI writes the code. You provide the supply chain logic.** The assessable skill is not programming -- it is designing systems that solve real operational problems and explaining how they work.

The capstone Mega Exam uses a **completely different company** (Kunzler's cafe) to test whether you can transfer your skills to a new context.

## Practice Company

**Peak Fuel Foods** -- 5 products, 33 ingredients, 24 suppliers, 143 sales transactions. Read Maria's story in [readings/founders-story.md](readings/founders-story.md) to understand the business.
